Concurrency is when two or more tasks can start, run and complete in overlapping time periods.
It does not necessarely mean that they are running at the same instant.
For example, multitasking on a single core unit

Example; Concurency is like two lines of customers ordering from the same cashier.
	 Lines take turns ordering.

Parallelism is when tasks litteraly run at the same time. 

Example; Two lines of customers ordering from two different cashiers.
